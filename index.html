About Leaf ($LEAVES)

Leaf is a reflection-based, deflationary token built on Ladychain (EVM). Every transaction contributes to the long-term health of the ecosystem through automatic burns and reflections distributed directly to holders.

By combining DeFi mechanics with a nature-inspired theme, Leaf aims to create an accessible and rewarding experience for both early adopters and long-term believers. Holders can stake their $LEAVES in multiple pools, each with different lockup periods and APRs, to grow their position over time.

The vision is simple: as the forest grows, every leaf becomes more valuable.
