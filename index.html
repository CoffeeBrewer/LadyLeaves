About CoffeeHolics

CoffeeHolics is a café-inspired, reflection-based token built on Ladychain (EVM). Every transaction helps roast $BEANS: part of each transfer is burned and part is served back to holders as reflections.

With multiple staking pools themed around your favorite coffee styles, CoffeeHolics offers a cozy DeFi experience that rewards both casual sippers and long-term hodlers. Stake your $BEANS, earn extra rewards, and watch your balance grow as the café fills up.

The idea is simple: the longer you stay in the CoffeeHolics café, the more $BEANS you collect.
