About CoffeeHolics

CoffeeHolics is a café-inspired DeFi project built on Ladychain (EVM), centered around the $BEANS token. Instead of flashing charts and aggressive trading dashboards, CoffeeHolics aims to feel like your favorite coffee place: warm, calm, and quietly productive.

Every transaction in $BEANS applies a simple mechanic: a small portion is burned forever, reducing the total supply, while another portion is redistributed back to holders as reflections — loyalty beans for everyone who sticks around.

On top of that, CoffeeHolics offers multiple staking pools themed after iconic coffee styles. From flexible Espresso staking for active users, to longer Latte and Cold Brew locks for patient CoffeeHolics, each pool comes with its own rhythm and reward profile.

The vision is simple: turn checking your wallet into a familiar ritual. Brew your yield over time, keep an eye on the CoffeeBar dashboard, and grow together with the Beanfolk who call this little corner of Ladychain home.
